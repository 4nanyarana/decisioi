About Decisio

Purpose

Decisio was created to eliminate decision fatigue. Whether you're struggling to choose what to eat, what movie to watch, or what task to tackle next, Decisio makes the process effortless. Stop overthinking and just spin it!

Features

  • Dynamic Shuffle Box for randomized decisions
  • Quick Presets for common categories (Food, Movies, Study)
  • Decision History tracking to reflect on your choices
  • Regret Tracker to measure your satisfaction rate

Technologies Used

  • Frontend: Vanilla HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Backend & Auth: Firebase Firestore & Authentication
  • Typography: Google Fonts (Outfit)
